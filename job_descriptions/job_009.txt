Company Profile
Our client is a rapidly scaling digital finance platform built on modern blockchain infrastructure. The company serves a global user base and is backed by leading investors.
Job Description
The Role
As a senior Product leader, you will own the global identity, KYC/AML, and compliance platform. The role focuses on building high-scale onboarding, verification, and anti-financial-crime systems that balance user experience with regulatory rigor.
What You'll Do
Lead end-to-end KYC/CDD product: onboarding, verification, sanctions screening & risk scoring
Manage and optimize multiple vendors (e.g., SumSub, Onfido, Jumio, Chainalysis, ComplyAdvantage)
Build AML & transaction monitoring workflows with rules, alerts, and automation
Translate regulatory requirements into scalable product and engineering solutions
Improve internal compliance & case-management tooling
Stay ahead of global regulations and ensure readiness for new markets
Ideal Candidate
You have at least 10 years of experience in Product Management, with at least 3 years specializing in identity/compliance/KYC/AML
Experience building regulatory-grade compliance or risk systems in fintech/crypto/banking
Strong stakeholder communication across Compliance, Legal & Engineering
Hands-on working with major KYC/AML vendors & data providers
High-velocity, execution-focused mindset with strong ownership
The offer
Build mission-critical compliance infrastructure
High autonomy, fast execution environment
Competitive salary + meaningful equity
