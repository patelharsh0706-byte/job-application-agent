Company Profile
CED (CLOUDENGINE DIGITAL) is a global one-stop IT service and solutions platform, providing customers with a unified platform for project implementation and maintenance services in data center scenarios. We offer cutting-edge infrastructure solutions for enterprises seeking reliable and efficient technologies. We are committed to innovation and excellence, providing comprehensive products and services designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers.
Job Description
Responsibilities：
-Support sales by preparing standardized quotations based on customer requirements.
-Coordinate with product and supply chain teams to confirm configurations, inventory, and delivery schedules.
-Maintain pricing tools, quotation templates, and product documents.
-Track order and delivery progress to ensure smooth execution.
-Follow up on after-sales cases (warranty, repair, DOA) and provide timely customer updates.
-Collect product and service feedback for continuous improvement.
Requirements：
-Good command of Excel.
-Strong learning ability and interest in server/IT products.
-Good communication skills, detail-oriented, responsible.
-IT background preferred.
