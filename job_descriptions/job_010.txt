Company Profile

Job Description
The Role
As a senior Product Manager in the risk and fraud prevention platform, you will build intelligent, multi-layered systems that protect users and maintain platform integrity while enabling a seamless experience for legitimate activity.
What You'll Do
Own the roadmap for risk, fraud detection, and platform integrity
Integrate and manage leading fraud-prevention vendors and tools
Define and deploy risk rules, alerts, scoring models, and monitoring flows
Analyse behavioural patterns and threat signals to identify emerging attack vectors
Build clear user experiences for customers impacted by risk controls
Improve internal workflows for investigations, dispute handling, and resolution
Partner with Engineering, Data, and Ops to deliver scalable risk solutions across markets
Ideal Candidate
You have at least 10 years of experience in Product Management, with a minimum of 3 years specializing in anti-fraud, user risk, identity theft, trust & safety, etc.
You have a strong understanding of common fraud patterns (ATO, synthetic IDs, phishing, behavioural attacks)
Experienced with major risk or fraud vendors (e.g., Sift, Sardine, Forter, etc.)
You are highly analytical, comfortable designing and testing risk logic and reviewing multi-source data
Clear communicator who can simplify complex decisions for technical and non-technical teams
High-ownership mindset, comfortable operating in fast-moving environments
The offer
Build a mission-critical risk platform at the core of a global technology product
High autonomy, fast execution environment
Competitive salary, meaningful equity
