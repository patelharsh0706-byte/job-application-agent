Company Profile
Join a high-traffic consumer digital platform in a mobile-first environment where you’ll play a hands-on role across platform operations, feature delivery, and user growth initiatives.
Job Description
Key Responsibilities:
Support day-to-day platform operations across mobile and web, ensuring stability and performance
Manage and groom the product backlog, including writing user stories and defining acceptance criteria
Translate business and user needs into clear, actionable product requirements
Partner closely with Engineering on sprint planning, release coordination, and delivery tracking
Coordinate feature enhancements, bug fixes, and launches with internal and external stakeholders
Assist in executing in-app growth and engagement initiatives to improve activation and retention
Support UAT, QA validation, and post-launch monitoring
Track product performance metrics and recommend data-driven improvements
Identify opportunities to improve user experience and operational efficiency
Ideal Candidate Profile
You have 3+ years of experience in Product, Platform Operations, Growth, or Digital Delivery roles
You have experience contributing to feature delivery within a consumer-facing digital product (mobile preferred)
You have exposure to Agile/Scrum environments and sprint-based execution
You have experience collaborating closely with Engineering teams and understanding technical constraints
You have a structured, analytical, and execution-focused mindset
You have strong attention to detail and a proactive ownership mentality
You have an ambitious, resilient, and agile personality
You have a strong hunger to learn, grow quickly, and take real ownership
What’s on Offer
High-ownership role with meaningful autonomy and decision-making responsibility
Direct impact on scaling and evolving a core consumer digital platform
Broad exposure across platform operations, growth initiatives, and cross-functional delivery
